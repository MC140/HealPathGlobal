Section 01

About This Policy

HealPath Global Concierge Inc. ("HealPath Global," "we," "us," or "our") is a corporation registered in Ontario, Canada. We provide medical tourism concierge services — coordinating access to internationally accredited hospitals in India for patients from Canada and the United States. We do not provide medical care, diagnosis, or clinical advice of any kind.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, retain, and protect your personal information in compliance with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), S.C. 2000, c. 5, and its associated regulations.

By using our Website or engaging our services, you consent to the practices described in this Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our Website or services.

Section 03

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Full name, email address, phone number, city, province or state, and country of residence
  • Medical information you voluntarily share: procedure type, medical history, prior diagnoses, surgical history, and medical records
  • Insurance status and coverage details (primarily for US patients)
  • Passport number and identification documents for medical visa guidance
  • Payment information to process your concierge fee (via Stripe — we do not store card numbers)
  • Records of all communications between you and HealPath Global
  • Signed service agreements, consent forms, and acknowledgment documents

Information Collected Automatically

  • Browser type, operating system, and device type
  • IP address and approximate geographic region
  • Pages visited, session duration, and referring URL
  • Cookies used to maintain basic site functionality

What We Do Not Collect

HealPath Global does not directly collect or store credit card or banking account numbers. All payment processing is conducted by Stripe, Inc. under its own PCI-DSS-compliant privacy practices.

Section 04

Why We Collect Your Information

HealPath Global collects personal information only for the specific, limited purposes below. We will not use your information for any other purpose without your prior consent.

  1. To match you with appropriate JCI-accredited hospitals and surgeons for your stated procedure
  2. To communicate with hospital International Patient Departments (in de-identified form during matching) to obtain quotes
  3. To transmit your full medical records to your selected hospital once you have signed the Service Agreement
  4. To coordinate travel, logistics, visa documentation, and accommodation on your behalf
  5. To process your concierge fee payment
  6. To provide on-call support during your stay in India
  7. To transmit post-operative records to your designated domestic physician upon your return
  8. To conduct 90-day post-return follow-up support
  9. To comply with our legal obligations under Ontario law and PIPEDA
  10. To monitor and improve service quality using anonymized, aggregated data
Section 05

Who We Share Your Information With

HealPath Global does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share it only as follows:

Hospitals Under Evaluation (De-Identified)

During the matching phase, we present your case to JCI-accredited hospitals using de-identified information only — procedure type, approximate age, and general medical history — without disclosing your name or identity until you have selected a hospital and signed the Service Agreement.

Your Selected Treating Hospital

Once you select a hospital and execute the Service Agreement, we transmit your full medical records and identity documentation to that hospital's International Patient Department to facilitate pre-operative assessment and scheduling.

Your Designated Domestic Physician

Upon your return home, we transmit complete post-operative records — operative reports, imaging, pathology, and discharge summaries — to the physician you designate.

Payment Processors

We use Stripe, Inc. for payment processing. Stripe's privacy practices are governed by stripe.com/privacy.

Technology Service Providers

We use limited third-party providers for encrypted file sharing, digital signatures, CRM, and email hosting. All are bound by confidentiality obligations.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or lawful court order. We will notify you of any such request to the extent permitted by law.

Section 06

How We Protect Your Information

  • All medical records and sensitive documents are transmitted and stored exclusively via end-to-end encrypted file-sharing services. Standard email is never used for this purpose.
  • Signed agreements and patient documents are stored in access-controlled, password-protected systems.
  • Access to personal health information is restricted to HealPath Global personnel on a strict need-to-know basis.
  • We maintain a documented Data Breach Response Plan. In the event of a breach posing real risk of significant harm, we will notify affected individuals and the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada as required by PIPEDA.
  • We do not store your information on public cloud services without encryption at rest.
If you believe your personal information has been accessed without authorization, contact our Privacy Officer immediately at [email protected].
Section 07

How Long We Keep Your Information

HealPath Global retains personal information for seven (7) years from the date of your last service transaction. This reflects standard Ontario business record-keeping obligations and allows for resolution of post-service inquiries, complaints, or legal matters.

After the retention period, personal information is destroyed using secure deletion methods. Physical documents are shredded; digital files are permanently and securely deleted.

Section 09

Cross-Border Data Transfers

By engaging our services, you acknowledge and consent to the following necessary cross-border transfers:

  • To India: Your medical records and identity documents will be transmitted to your selected JCI-accredited hospital. Indian hospitals are subject to the Medical Council of India and, where applicable, JCI and NABH accreditation standards.
  • To the United States: Our technology service providers (including Stripe and cloud infrastructure) may process or store data in the US under contractual data protection obligations.

HealPath Global takes reasonable steps to ensure that personal information transferred internationally receives a comparable level of protection to that afforded under PIPEDA.

Section 10

Cookies

Our website uses cookies solely to maintain basic site functionality (e.g., session management). We do not use cookies for behavioural advertising, cross-site tracking, or user profiling. You may disable cookies in your browser settings; however, some website features may not function as intended.

Section 11

Children's Privacy

HealPath Global's services are intended for adults seeking elective or semi-urgent medical procedures. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18 without parental or guardian consent. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, contact our Privacy Officer immediately.
