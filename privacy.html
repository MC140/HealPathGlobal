Section 02

Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all personal information collected by HealPath Global through our website, consultation intake form, email correspondence, phone consultations, coordination and delivery of our concierge services, and post-return follow-up communications.

This Policy does not apply to the privacy practices of hospitals, surgeons, or other third-party service providers we work with. Those parties have their own privacy policies.

HealPath Global is subject to Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and applicable provincial privacy laws.

Section 04

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information only for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiry and determine whether HealPath Global's services are appropriate for your situation
  • To identify suitable JCI-accredited hospitals and surgeons for your stated procedure
  • To obtain quotes and coordinate with hospital International Patient Departments
  • To assist with visa documentation, travel arrangements, and accommodation
  • To provide in-country support during your medical stay
  • To transmit your medical records and operative reports to your domestic physician after return
  • To conduct 90-day post-return follow-up contact
  • To process payment for our concierge fee
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations
  • To improve our services based on anonymised, aggregated feedback
We will never use your personal health information for marketing purposes, sell it to third parties, or share it with any party not directly involved in your care coordination.

Section 06

International Transfers of Personal Information

Because HealPath Global coordinates care in India, your personal and medical information will necessarily be transferred to the selected hospital in India. By engaging HealPath Global's services and signing the Patient Service Agreement, you explicitly consent to this transfer.

Indian hospitals we work with are JCI-accredited and subject to Indian law regarding patient privacy and medical records. We require all hospitals in our network to maintain appropriate data security standards.

When your information is transferred outside Canada, it becomes subject to the laws of the receiving country. We take reasonable steps to ensure appropriate protections are in place, but cannot guarantee the same level of protection as under PIPEDA.

Section 08

How We Protect Your Information

  • All medical records are transferred using encrypted file-sharing services — never by standard unencrypted email
  • Our website uses HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Access to patient records is restricted to HealPath Global staff directly involved in your coordination
  • Payment processing is handled by Stripe, which maintains PCI DSS Level 1 compliance — HealPath Global never stores full payment card details
  • Physical documents containing personal information are stored securely and destroyed by secure shredding when no longer required
No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we use commercially reasonable safeguards, we cannot guarantee absolute security. In the event of a data breach affecting your rights, we will notify you and the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada as required by PIPEDA.
