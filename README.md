# HealPath Global

**World-Class Surgery. A Fraction of the Cost.**

HealPath Global is an Ontario-registered medical tourism concierge that helps Canadians and Americans access JCI-accredited surgery in India — safely, verifiably, and affordably.

---

## What We Do

HealPath Global coordinates the entire medical travel journey — from finding the right hospital, to travel logistics, to helping patients hand their records back to their doctor at home.

- **Hospital Matching** — JCI-accredited hospitals only
- **Surgeon Verification** — Credentials independently verified
- **Travel Coordination** — Visa, flights, accommodation, transfers
- **Post-Return Care** — 90-day follow-up with domestic physician
